FILOKSENIA

"friend to a stranger"

At Filoksenia Greek Cousine, our goal is to provide our customers with outstanding sustainably sourced Greek meals. Over three quarters of all food used in our restaurant is grown or raised in the state of Illinois. As our name implies, service is a top priority at Filoksenia.

BREAKFAST

Sfakianopita

Sfakianopita, or "sweet pie" is a traditional Greek breakfast similar to crepes or pancakes. The thin layers of dough are combined with a soft whey cheese.

Strapatsada

Strapatsada, AKA "egg dish" consists of refreshing flavors from locally sources eggs, tomatoes, fresh herbs, and feta cheese.

Ladenia

Ladenia is a Greek flatbread topped with tomatoes, onions, and olive oil. Ladenia is believed to be a Greek version of the Italian pizza.

Rizogalo

Rizogalo is a Greek-style rice pudding, which can be enhanced with your choice of vanilla or citrus.

Omeletta

Though "Omeletta" sounds similar to the well known omlette, it is actually more similar to a fritata. The omletta consists of fried poatoes and eggs with bell peppers.

Bougatsa

Bougatsa is a sweet, layered pastry filled with your choice of minced meat, cheese, or semolina custard.

LUNCH

Gyros

Gyros consists of lamb meat, tomato, onions, fried potatos, and tzatziki wrapped into a pita bread.

Souvlaki

Your choice of chicken or pork grilled on a skewer. Served with pita bread, vegetables, and tzatsiki on the side.

Stifado

Stifado is a Greek stew made of locally sourced beef, onions, wine, and tomatoes.

Spanakopita

Spanakopita is a Greek spinach pie filled with fresh spinach, leeks, herds, feta, and olive oil.

DINNER

Gemista

Gemnista is stuffed tomatoes or peppers filled with rice and herbs.

Moussaka

Similar to an American casserole, moussaka is baked in the oven made of sautéed aubergine, minced lamb, fried puréed tomato, potato, onion, garlic and spices like cinnamon and allspice. It is topped with creamy béchamel sauce and cheese.

Kleftiko

Kleftiko is slow cooked lamb with potatos and vegetables.

Makaronia me kima

Makaronia me kima is made with spaghetti noodles in a minced meat and tomato sauce.

Yiouvetsi

Yiouvetsi is a dish made with lamb, orzo pasta, onions, garlic, red wine, and tomato sauce. Served with grated cheese on top.

Fasolatha

Fasolatha is a white bean soup consisting of beans, crushed tomatoes, onions, carrots and celery.
