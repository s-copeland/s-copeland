Why take photos?

  1. To capture memories.
  2. To express oneself.
  3. To appreciate the little things.

But I can do that with my iPhone...

Although the cameras on smartphones have developed a significant amount in the past decade, they are still incomparable to DSLRs and mirrorless cameras. While phones are great for taking a quick snapshot of the family to post on Facebook, DSLRs and mirrorless cameras open the door to additional creativity, longer focal length, and further control over the settings.
