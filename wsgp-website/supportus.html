Why Support Us?

White Shepherd Genetics Project is dedicated to researching and improving the health of the White Shepherd breed. As a registered 501c3 Non-Profit organization, your donation is text deductible (EIN 30-0376245). If you love the White Shepherd breed, please consider donating. Our organization survives off of donations and fundraisers alone.
